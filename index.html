Introduction


Welcome to our food resource production website! We are dedicated to promoting sustainable food production and reducing food waste in order to create a more equitable and sustainable food system for all. Our mission is to provide resources and education to individuals, communities, and businesses to help them make informed decisions about food production, distribution, and consumption. Our goal is to ensure that everyone has access to healthy, nutritious, and sustainably produced food, while also reducing the environmental impact of food production and waste. Through our website, you will find a wealth of information on sustainable agriculture, food waste reduction, food safety, and much more.

About


Food Resource Production is a company dedicated to promoting sustainable food production and reducing food waste. Our mission is to create a more equitable and sustainable food system for all. We believe that food is a basic human right, and everyone should have access to healthy, affordable, and nutritious food. We work with farmers, food processors, and other stakeholders in the food industry to promote sustainable agricultural practices that are environmentally friendly, socially responsible, and economically viable. Our goal is to ensure that food is produced in a way that protects natural resources, preserves biodiversity, and minimizes waste. Most food produced for the food industry comes from commodity crops using conventional agricultural practices. Agriculture is the process of producing food, feeding products, fiber and other desired products by the cultivation of certain plants and the raising of domesticated animals (livestock). On average, 83% of the food consumed by humans is produced using terrestrial agriculture.[8] Other food sources include aquaculture and fishing.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
Launched in 2016

This is a crop insurance scheme that provides financial support to farmers in case of crop damage due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases. The scheme covers all food crops and horticultural crops.


Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)
Launched in 2015

This is a scheme that promotes organic farming and sustainable agriculture practices. It provides financial support to farmers to adopt organic farming methods and encourages the use of natural inputs like compost, manure, and bio-fertilizers.


National Food Security Mission (NFSM)
Launched in 2007

This is a scheme that aims to increase the production of food grains like rice, wheat, and pulses. It provides financial support to farmers for various activities like seed distribution, soil health management, and farm mechanization.
